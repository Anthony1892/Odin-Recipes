Grilled Utaka

Lake Malawis dried fresh water fish populary known as Utaka

This type of fish is prefered by most malawians to be dried and grilled on makala(charcoal)for its flavor and aroma

Ingridients

  • Utaka fish
  • Salt
  • Kambuzi pepper(preferably)
  • 3 tbspns of water

Steps

  1. Ready some charcoal fire
  2. Put 3 tbspns of water in a small container and add some salt
  3. Crush some kambuzi papper into the salt water mixture if you like
  4. Place a wire ontop the fire
  5. Dip the utaka in the mixture and place it on the wire
  6. Grill side by side untill it start gaining some brown color
