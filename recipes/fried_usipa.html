Fried Usipa

Usipa is a sadine-like fish found in lake malawi

This fish is delicious in any way someone would want to prepare it, but here we will explain about fried usipa.

Ingridients

  • Fresh usipa
  • Fresh size tomato
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Heat your stove
  2. clean your pan and heat it to remove any eccess water
  3. Pour cooking oil on the pan
  4. When the oil has heated(not too hot), put in usipa, do't worry if you deep fry or not for as long as the usipa is frying thats all
