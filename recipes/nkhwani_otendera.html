Nkhwani Otendera

One of the best dish for vegiterians.

Boiled fresh pumpkin leaves supplimented by groundnuts flour and tomatoes for color and flavor.

Ingridients

  • Fresh clean pumpkin leaves
  • 1lt water
  • groundnuts flour
  • Fresh sized ripe tomato
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Switch on you stove and pour abot 500ml water in a clean pot and put it on the stove to boil
  2. Cut you vegitables to size not too thin yet not too thick
  3. When the water boils put in your vegies in the pot and cover
  4. After the vegies have subsized turn them, upside down
  5. Cut in the tomatoe and add groundnuts flour about 2rd of a cup
  6. Leave to boil some more, add a little water if necessary
  7. After 5 minutes, stir thoughrouly to even the contents then you are done
