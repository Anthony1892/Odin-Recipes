Roasted Chambo

Fine malawis fresh lake signature fish

This fish is roasted on charcoal burning mildly to preserve the freshness of the fish.

You can prepare it plainly as it is from the water or you can add some spices to your liking.

Ingridients

  • Fresh chambo fish
  • Salt
  • Lemon
  • Charcoal

Step

  1. Clean the fish: srape the scales and remove trash from its abdomen
  2. Lay it somewhere clean and make some cuts across the fish, just deep enough
  3. Clean the lemon, cut it across and squeeze the juice onto the fish on both sides
  4. Leave it for sometime for the juice to filter in
  5. Prepare the braii stand and make fire
  6. When the charcoal is red hot you can start grilling your fish, if the fire seems too much splash some water on it just to ease the heat
  7. Grill side by side till light brown, then its ready
